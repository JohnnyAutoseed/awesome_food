## APIs

* [Chomp](https://chompthis.com/api/) - Real-time nutrition data and grocery item search by barcode.
* [Edamam](https://developer.edamam.com/) - High-quality API for nutrition analysis, recipe search, and food database.
* [Open Food Facts](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/data) - The "Wikipedia of food"—a massive, community-driven database of products and ingredients.
* [Spoonacular](https://spoonacular.com/food-api) - Comprehensive API for recipes, meal planning, and detailed nutritional breakdown.
* [TheMealDB](https://www.themealdb.com/api.php) - An open, crowd-sourced database of recipes from around the world.

## Datasets

* [FoodData Central (USDA)](https://fdc.nal.usda.gov/) - The definitive scientific source for food and nutrient data from the United States.
* [Global Food Research Program](https://www.globalfoodresearchprogram.org/resources/data-and-methods/) - Data resources focusing on food policy, labeling, and global nutrition.
* [Wegmans Recipe Dataset](https://github.com/TofunmiS/Wegmans-Recipe-Dataset) - A structured dataset containing thousands of recipes from the popular grocery chain.

## Open Source Projects

* [Grocy](https://grocy.info/) - An ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) solution for your kitchen to manage groceries and household tasks.
* [Mealie](https://mealie.io/) - A self-hosted recipe manager and meal planner with a clean REST API.
* [Tandoor Recipes](https://tandoor.dev/) - A full-stack recipe management application with meal planning and shopping lists.

## Visuals and Design

* [Foodiesfeed](https://www.foodiesfeed.com/) - Thousands of high-quality, free-to-use food photos for developers and designers.
